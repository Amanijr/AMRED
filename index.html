About Us

AMRED Health Center is a Tanzanian-owned healthcare facility established in June 2021 and registered in compliance with Tanzania’s health laws and regulations. Located in Mbweni Teta, Kinondoni Municipality, Dar es Salaam, the center provides affordable and quality healthcare services.

  • Our Mission

    To provide accessible, affordable, and comprehensive healthcare services that meets community needs through qualified professionals, modern facilities, and a strong focus on preventive and health education.

  • Our Vision

    To be a trusted and leading community health centre in Tanzania, delivering accessible, high-quality, and compassionate health care that improves The well being of individuals and communities

Services

We offer a wide range of healthcare services designed to meet the diverse needs of our patients.

Dental Services

We offer dental checkups and treatment for tooth pain, decay, and gum problems.

Theatre Services

We offer safe surgical services for emergency and planned operations.

OPD – Outpatient Services

We offer everyday medical consultations for common and chronic illnesses.

IPD – Inpatient Services

We offer ward admission and close monitoring for patients who need extended care.

RCH – Reproductive and Child Health

We offer reproductive health services including maternal care, child health, and family planning.

Maternity Services

We offer comprehensive maternity care including prenatal, delivery, and postnatal services.

Pharmacy Services

We provide a wide range of medications and health products to meet your needs.

Laboratory Services

We offer a range of diagnostic tests and health screenings to support accurate medical care.

Radiology Services

We provide imaging services including Ultrasound to aid in diagnosis and treatment.

Departments

We offer a wide range of departments to provide healthcare services.

  • Dental
  • Theatre
  • OutPatient (OPD)
  • InPatient (IPD)
  • Reproductive and Child Health (RCH)
  • Maternity
  • Pharmacy
  • Laboratory
  • Radiology

Dental

  • Dental examinations and consultations
  • Tooth extraction
  • Dental fillings and restorations
  • Root canal treatment (basic level)
  • Scaling and polishing
  • Emergency dental care for trauma
  • Minor oral surgeries
  • Denture and prosthetic services

Theatre

  • Elective and emergency surgeries
  • Minor and major surgical procedures

OPD – Outpatient Department

  • General medical consultations
  • Specialist clinics (e.g., Pediatrics, Eye, Dental)
  • Chronic disease management (e.g., diabetes, hypertension)
  • Emergency triage and referral
  • Minor procedures (e.g., wound care, injections)
  • Health screenings and preventive care

IPD – Inpatient Department

  • Admission and ward care
  • Medical ward
  • Surgical ward
  • Paediatric ward
  • Monitoring and nursing services

RCH – Reproductive & Child Health

  • Antenatal care (ANC)
  • Postnatal care (PNC)
  • Immunization services
  • Growth monitoring and nutrition
  • PMTCT services
  • Family planning

Maternity

  • Labour and delivery services
  • High-risk pregnancy monitoring and referral
  • Caesarean section
  • Neonatal services
  • Fetal monitoring
  • Post-delivery care
  • Obstetric emergency care

Pharmacy

  • Dispensing of medicines
  • Counselling on drug use

Laboratory

  • Hematology (CBC etc.)
  • Biochemistry (LFT, RFT, glucose, lipids)
  • Parasitology (Malaria, stool, urine)
  • HIV testing
  • Blood group & cross-matching
  • Sample collection and storage

Radiology

  • Ultrasound services
